Getting Here

Navigate the NYC subway with the official MTA app: App Store · Google Play

From JFK

Taxi / Rideshare — ~45–60 min, ~$60–85 (tolls included). Driving directions →

Fastest transit — AirTrain to Jamaica → LIRR to Penn Station → F train to 2nd Ave (~60–70 min total, 1 block walk). Transit directions →

Fewest transfers — AirTrain to Jamaica → E train direct to Delancey/Essex St → walk (~75–90 min, 1 transfer).

From LaGuardia (LGA)

Taxi / Rideshare — ~30–45 min, ~$40–60. Recommended — LGA has no direct subway access. Driving directions →

Fastest transit — Q70 SBS bus to Jackson Heights–Roosevelt Ave → E/F train to Delancey/Essex St (~55–70 min, 1 transfer). Transit directions →

Fewest transfers — M60 SBS bus to 125th St → 6 train to Bleecker St → walk (~70–80 min, 1 transfer). Transit from LGA always requires at least one bus leg — taxi is the easier option.

From Newark (EWR)

Taxi / Rideshare — ~45–60 min, ~$70–110 (NJ surcharges apply). Driving directions →

Fastest transit — AirTrain Newark → NJ Transit to Penn Station → F train to 2nd Ave (~60–75 min, 1 block walk). Transit directions →

Fewest transfers — Same route as above (AirTrain + NJ Transit = 1 transfer, then F train). Purchase a NJ Transit ticket for the train leg — MetroCard / OMNY is not accepted on NJ Transit.
